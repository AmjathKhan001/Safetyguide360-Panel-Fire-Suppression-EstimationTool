Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

FireCalc Pro is a client-side application. We do not store any of your personal information on our servers. All data entered into the calculator (including panel dimensions, user information, and project details) is processed entirely in your browser.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect or store your data, we don't use it for any purpose. Your information remains on your device and is only used for:

  • Performing calculations for fire suppression systems
  • Generating quotations locally on your device
  • Creating download files (PDF/CSV) on your computer

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: To understand how our tool is being used (anonymous data only)
  • Google AdSense: To display relevant advertisements
  • Buy Me a Coffee: For voluntary user support

4. Cookies

We use cookies only for:

  • Google Analytics (to track anonymous usage statistics)
  • Google AdSense (to show relevant ads)
  • No personal identification cookies are used

5. Data Security

Since we don't collect or store your data, there's no risk of data breaches from our servers. All your calculations and quotations remain on your local device.

6. Children's Privacy

Our tool is intended for professional use by adults. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our website.
